Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the app.

How to Delete Your Account

1. Open the [Your App Name] app
2. Go to Settings
3. Tap "Account"
4. Tap "Delete Account"
5. Confirm your decision
⚠️ Important: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your training data, progress, and account information will be permanently deleted.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account credentials
  • Training history and progress
  • All personal information

Need Help?
